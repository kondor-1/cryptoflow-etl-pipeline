# Why pin versions (>=x.x)?
#   Pinning ensures anyone who clones your repo gets a compatible version.
#   Not pinning at all means a new release could silently break your pipeline.
#   For a portfolio project, >= (minimum version) is fine. In production you'd
#   pin exact versions (==x.x.x) and use a lock file.
